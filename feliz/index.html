Samsung CSR I Khong Gian Chia Se S.hub

Developed and sponsored by Samsung, S.hub is a co-working space which aims to bring a comfortable, professional and supportive environment for young people.

S.hub is now available for public access in Hochiminh City, Da Nang, Hanoi.

Within the framework of activities at S.hub, Level Up Contest is a challenge that encourage young people to self-equip and self-improve their soft skills for a bright career.

As a full-service retainer for S.hub, we develop communication & operation plan with series of O-2-O activities, launching events, seasonal events, website coding & maintenance, Level Up contest online & offline...

Samsung CSR I Tech Institute

Bridging the gap between theorical and practical study, targeting to provide certified technicians in cell phone repairing, Samsung Tech Institute brings up opportunities for students of vocational schools and colleges to become skillful apprentice at the Service Centers.

This program strives to transform and train these students not only technical expertise but also soft skills required to meet the practical requirements of the future of work.

Our tasks are to support the offline engagement activities so that they can take a chance to practice one-by-one with customer and accumulate work experience through the problem solving at extracurricular trips.

Samsung Love Care
I Employee Volunteer Program

Believe in the power of education and the significance of healthcare, all activities of Samsung Employee Volunteer Program focus on elevate self-protection from sex abuse & environment protection training; organizing offline engagement related to Mobile Library, colorful interaction & entertainment; renovating the infrastructure and equipment at some primary schools; and free health checking up & giving a taste of their own medicine for the poor & children.

Our SOW are to make things happened: from communication to operation. Handling from internal recruitment, external partners, engagement activities & operation management to content package, visual material production, creative design and digital platforms; we desire to not only support but also be a companion to social responsibility actions.

Samsung’s technology helps learnings and Samsung’s employees, Love Care program, desires to bring happiness, caring, and love to those who are in the difficult circumstances through the practical activities.
